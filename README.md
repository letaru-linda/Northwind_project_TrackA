# Northwind SQL Business Intelligence Project
## Project Overview

This project contains a comprehensive SQL analysis performed on the Northwind dataset. The goal of the project was to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights using SQL.

The analysis focused on:

* Sales performance analysis
* Customer behavior analysis
* Employee productivity measurement
* Revenue trend analysis
* Inventory and reorder management
* Geographic sales analysis
* Executive KPI reporting

The project demonstrates practical SQL skills commonly used in:

* Data Analytics
* Business Intelligence
* Reporting and Dashboarding
* Sales Analytics
* Operational Analysis

---

# Problem Statement

Businesses generate large volumes of transactional data daily, but raw data alone does not provide meaningful business insights.

The objective of this project was to answer critical business questions such as:

* Which customers generate the highest revenue?
* Which employees contribute the most sales?
* Which product categories are growing or declining?
* Which products require inventory attention?
* Which geographic regions generate the highest revenue?
* How efficient are order deliveries?
* Which customers are loyal, regular, or occasional buyers?
* What are the overall business KPIs executives should monitor?

Using SQL, the project transformed operational data into strategic business intelligence reports.

# Key Business Analyses Performed

## 1. Customer Revenue Analysis

Calculated total revenue generated by each customer.

### Insight

Identified the highest-value customers contributing the largest share of company revenue.

---

## 2. Employee Performance Analysis

Measured employee order processing, revenue contribution, and delivery performance.

### Insight

Revealed top-performing employees and operational efficiency levels.

---

## 3. Revenue Trend Analysis

Analyzed monthly and quarterly revenue trends using CTEs and window functions.

### Insight

Identified growth patterns, revenue fluctuations, and seasonality trends.

---

## 4. Product Performance Analysis

Ranked products by revenue and identified best-selling products.

### Insight

Highlighted the most profitable products and categories.

---

## 5. Customer Segmentation

Segmented customers into Loyal, Regular, and Occasional buyers.

### Insight

Showed that regular customers generated the highest portion of revenue.

---

## 6. Geographic Revenue Analysis

Analyzed revenue by customer country.

### Insight

Identified the strongest geographic markets and high-performing regions.

---

## 7. Inventory & Reorder Analysis

Created product reorder reports and inventory urgency flags.

### Insight

Helped identify products requiring inventory planning and reorder attention.

---

## 8. Delivery Performance Analysis

Measured delivery efficiency and on-time shipping rates.

### Insight

Highlighted operational strengths and delivery delays.

---

## 9. Executive KPI Dashboard

Built a CEO-level summary report using UNION ALL.

### Insight

Provided a high-level business overview containing:

* Total Revenue

* Best Selling Product
* Top Customer
* Top Employee

* Total Customers

# Key Insights From the Analysis

## Revenue Insights

* A small number of customers generated a large percentage of total revenue.
* Certain product categories consistently outperformed others.
* Revenue trends showed seasonal fluctuations.

## Customer Insights

* Regular customers contributed significantly to revenue.
* Some customers had never placed orders, indicating churn risk.
* Customer segmentation helped identify valuable customer groups.

## Employee Insights

* Revenue contribution varied significantly across employees.
* Some employees processed substantially more orders.
* Delivery performance differed between employees.

## Product Insights

* Some products consistently ranked among the best sellers.
* Certain product categories showed declining performance.
* Inventory analysis revealed products needing reorder attention.

## Operational Insights

* Delivery efficiency could be monitored through shipping analysis.
* Fraud-style ordering patterns could be detected using SQL.
* Geographic revenue analysis revealed top-performing markets.
